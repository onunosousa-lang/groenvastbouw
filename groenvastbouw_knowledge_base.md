## What is a Passive House?

A Passive House uses **up to 90% less energy** for heating and cooling compared to traditional homes.

### Energy Cost Savings (100m² home in Netherlands)

| Cost Category | Traditional Home | Passive House | Your Savings |
|---------------|------------------|---------------|--------------|
| Heating | €1,800/year | €300/year | €1,500/year |
| Cooling | €500/year | €100/year | €400/year |
| Ventilation | €200/year | €100/year | €100/year |
| **TOTAL** | **€2,500/year** | **€500/year** | **€2,000/year** |

**Over 30 years:** €60,000 in energy savings  
**Plus:** Increased property value (+5-10%) = €8,000-€16,000  
**Total benefit:** €68,000-€76,000
